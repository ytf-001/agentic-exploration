## ⚠️ CRITICAL NOTE: LINCOLN COUNTY MORATORIUM

Lincoln County imposed a **moratorium on commercial and utility-grade wind and solar energy development** beginning in 2020, extended in October 2023 through at least **April 23, 2025**. The county has been developing an updated comprehensive plan and revised zoning regulations for renewable energy. As of early 2025, commissioners were finalizing updated regulations that include decommissioning requirements, financial assurance, setback revisions, and transmission/access easement plans. **Before proceeding, confirm the current moratorium status and newly adopted regulations with the Lincoln County Planning Department.**

## KEY RISKS & RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **🚨 Moratorium Status**: Lincoln County's moratorium on commercial wind/solar was set to expire April 2025. Confirm it has been lifted and that new regulations are in effect before investing significant development capital.

2. **🦅 Lesser Prairie-Chicken**: This species is the single most significant biological constraint. Early engagement with USFWS and a comprehensive habitat assessment is essential. Consider proactive enrollment in a Candidate Conservation Agreement or participation in the range-wide HCP.

3. **⚡ Transmission Constraints**: Eastern Colorado's grid is near capacity. Secure an interconnection queue position as early as possible and evaluate whether Tri-State's or WAPA's planned upgrades will create new capacity at a feasible point of interconnection.

4. **📋 Evolving State Legislation**: Colorado has been actively considering legislation (e.g., SB 24-212 and related bills) that could preempt or limit local government authority over renewable energy siting. Monitor the Colorado General Assembly for changes that could affect Lincoln County's regulatory authority.

5. **💧 New State Wetland Program**: Colorado's new dredge-and-fill program (HB 24-1379 / Regulation 87) is newly implemented. Even areas that may not contain federally jurisdictional waters could now require state permits for impacts to ephemeral drainages and playas common on the eastern plains.
